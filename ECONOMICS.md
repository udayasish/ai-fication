# ECONOMICS.md — Unit Economics

## What is a converted lead worth to Credex?

Credex sells AI credits and consulting to startups. A "converted lead" = a startup that books a consultation and purchases credits.

**Reasoning:**

- Average startup team size hitting this tool: 10 people
- Average AI tool spend per team([Atlanta Fed Policy Hub Macroblog, Mar 2026]): ~$400/mo ($4,800/year)
  Source: (https://www.atlantafed.org/research-and-data/publications/policy-hub-macroblog/2026/03/25/how-might-ai-change-the-workplace-evidence-from-corporate-executives)
- Credex consultation converts to credit purchase: assume $500–$2,000 one-time or recurring
- Conservative LTV(Lifetime Value) of one converted customer can be: **$1,200** (year 1)
- If Credex retains the customer: $1,200 × 2 years = **$2,400 LTV**

## Conversion funnel

```
Visitors land on page
        ↓
Complete audit (fill form + submit)    → 40% conversion (low friction, no login)
        ↓
View results page                      → 100% of completions
        ↓
Submit email (lead capture)            → 25% of results viewers
        ↓
Open email + click report link         → 40% email open rate
        ↓
Book Credex consultation (>$500 saves) → 10% of high-savings leads
        ↓
Purchase credits                       → 60% of consultations
```

**End-to-end: 100 visitors → 40 audits → 10 leads → 1 consultation → 0.6 credit purchase**

Blended lead-to-purchase: ~6%

## What needs to be true for $1M ARR(Annual Recurring Revenue) in 18 months

**$1M ARR = ~$83,000/month revenue**

At $1,200 LTV per customer: need **~70 new paying customers/month** by month 18.

Working backwards:

- 70 purchases/month ÷ 0.6 close rate = **117 consultations/month**
- 117 consultations ÷ 0.10 consult rate = **1,170 high-savings leads/month**
- 1,170 high-savings leads = 30% of total leads → **3,900 total leads/month**
- 3,900 leads at 25% capture rate → **15,600 audits/month**
- 15,600 audits at 40% completion rate → **39,000 monthly visitors**

**39,000 monthly visitors by month 18 is achievable if:**

- The viral loop works (each shared audit card brings 2–3 new visitors)
- HackerNews/Reddit drives 2–3 spikes of 5,000+ visitors
- Credex runs 1 email campaign/month to its existing network
- SEO captures "AI tool comparison" and "cursor vs copilot" queries (3–6 month lag)

#### Phase 2 — Months 4–9: Social media presence ($500–1,500/mo budget)

- These days there are too many AI companies serving different types of AI services.
- So when a genuine lead want to buy a product/service, he might cross check if the company is legit. And one of the simple way to check is their LinkedIn and even in some cases Instagram handle.
- So it very important to be active on these handles to maximise genuine leads.

**LinkedIn:**

- Post 3x/week: audit result screenshots, "we found $X/mo in savings" case studies, AI tool comparison posts
- Target: engineering managers, CTOs, founders — exactly who uses LinkedIn professionally
- Estimated reach: 5,000–15,000 impressions/week organically with consistent posting
- Post blogs about different benchmarks of different AI Tools.
- Post about reports about AI Tools.
- Sponsored posts boosting top-performing content: $300–500/mo → est. 2,000–4,000 additional visitors/mo

**X/Twitter:**

- Post daily: tool comparisons, savings screenshots, reply to every "cursor vs copilot" thread
- Build audience of developers who follow AI tool discussions
- Estimated: 1,000–3,000 visitors/mo from organic + pinned audit link

**Total from social (month 6):** ~5,000–8,000 visitors/mo

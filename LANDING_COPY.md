## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes, completely. No credit card, no login, no trial. You fill in your tools, we run the audit, you see the results instantly. We capture your email only after you've seen the savings — never before.

**Q: How do you know the pricing data is accurate?**
A: Every number in the audit engine traces to an official vendor pricing page, verified as of submission week. Sources are documented in PRICING_DATA.md. We re-verify monthly.

**Q: Will you recommend switching to something worse just to show savings?**
A: No. The algorithm uses public benchmark data (SWE-bench, MMLU-Pro, GPQA Diamond) to measure quality alongside cost. We only recommend a switch if the alternative passes a quality threshold — we won't recommend a $5/month tool that scores 30 points lower on the benchmark.

**Q: What do you do with my email?**
A: We send you a copy of your audit report. For high-savings cases (>$500/mo), a Credex advisor may reach out about AI credits that can capture additional savings. You can unsubscribe from any email.

**Q: What tools do you support?**
A: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Windsurf, Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, and Gemini API. More tools added based on user requests.
